Output Rating System

How ORGCOMMAND measures performance — universal across all departments

The Formula

OUTPUT RATING = (Velocity × 0.4) + (Quality × 0.35) + (Responsiveness × 0.25)
40%
Velocity
Are they shipping? Tasks completed vs. tasks planned for this period.
10 = 120%+ 5 = 60% 1 = <30%
35%
Quality
Is the output good? Rework rate, bugs, revision cycles, error frequency.
10 = No rework 5 = Some 1 = Major
25%
Responsiveness
Do they pick up work fast? Time from assignment to progress, update frequency.
10 = Same day 5 = 2–3 days 1 = >5 days
8.0 – 10.0
Green — Strong
5.0 – 7.9
Yellow — Watch
1.0 – 4.9
Red — Intervene

Carlos's Red Rule — Applied to Output Rating

The Output Rating drives the traffic light. The Red Rule applies at the person level within a goal:

2 Greens in a row
Top performer flag. Consider for more responsibility.
2 Yellows in a row
Mandatory check-in. What's blocking them?
2 Reds in a row
Mandatory conversation. 3 reds = action plan or separation.
How Goal Health Is Calculated

Goal health is derived from two things:

1. Task Progress vs. Timeline
On Track: Task completion % ≥ time elapsed %
At Risk: Completion is 15%+ behind schedule
Blocked: Any task has been blocked for 48+ hours
2. Team Output Rating Average
Green: Average team OR ≥ 7.0 and no blockers
Yellow: Average OR 5.0–6.9 or minor delays
Red: Average OR < 5.0 or critical blockers

Whichever is worse wins. A goal with great people scores but blocked tasks is still blocked.
