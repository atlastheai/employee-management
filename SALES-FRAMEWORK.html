Key Insight Dev needs two tools (Jira for work, GitHub for output). Sales just needs the CRM. Deals are their tasks. Revenue is their output. Activity logs are their commits. It's all in one place.

What Are "Goals" for Sales?

Three types. Each maps directly to a Goal card in ORGCOMMAND:

Goal TypeWhat It IsExampleCadence
Quota Goal Revenue target for a period "Q1 Revenue — $1.2M" Monthly or Quarterly
Campaign Goal Strategic push with a defined outcome "LATAM Expansion — 5 POCs" One-time, time-bound
Account Goal Grow/retain specific accounts "Expand Enterprise Tier — 10 upsells" Quarterly

Most sales teams will have 1 quota goal always active + 1–2 campaign/account goals running alongside. That's 2–3 goals visible on the dashboard at any time.

Activities ≠ Tasks A common mistake: tracking calls, emails, demos as "tasks." They're not. They're activities within a deal. The deal is the unit of work. Activities are how you move the deal forward — like commits are how you move a ticket forward.

Why These Three (and Not Something Else)

ComponentWhat It CatchesWithout It You Miss…
Attainment Results. Did they close? The person who's busy but doesn't close
Pipeline Integrity Sustainability. Are deals real? The person who closes junk (heavy discounts, churn)
Activity Effort. Are they working? The person coasting on one big deal while pipeline dries up
The Lazy Closer Problem Without Pipeline Integrity, you can't tell the difference between a rep who closes $200K of solid deals and one who closes $200K by giving away 40% discounts on deals that churn in 60 days. Same attainment, wildly different value.

Sales Goal Health

Two inputs, whichever is worse wins — same logic as dev.

HealthRevenue PacePipeline Coverage
● On Track Closed revenue ≥ time-weighted target Pipeline ≥ 3× remaining target
● At Risk 15%+ behind pace Pipeline 1.5–3× remaining target
● Blocked 30%+ behind pace Pipeline < 1.5× remaining target
Pipeline Coverage Is the Leading Indicator Revenue closed is lagging — it tells you what already happened. Pipeline coverage tells you what's about to happen. If a rep is at 80% of quota but has <1.5× coverage on the remainder, they're not going to make it. Flag it now, not at month-end.
