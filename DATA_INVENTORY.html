📈 Per-Rep Metrics Available

1. Output Rating (0-10 Scale)

  • Overall Output Rating - Composite score of performance
  • Color Status - RED/YELLOW/GREEN classification
  • Velocity Score - How fast they move deals
  • Quality Score - Win rate and deal quality
  • Responsiveness Score - Lead response time

2. Deal Metrics

  • Deals Won - Number of closed deals
  • Deals Lost - Opportunities that didn't close
  • Win Rate - Percentage of deals closed
  • Deal Values - Revenue per deal
  • Pipeline Status - Active opportunities

3. Call Quality Metrics

  • Total Calls - Number of calls made
  • Call Duration - Time spent on calls
  • Ghost Call % - Calls under 5 seconds (gaming indicator)
  • Meaningful Call % - Calls over 30 seconds
  • Talk Time Distribution - Quality vs quantity analysis

4. Activity Metrics

  • Total Tasks - Tasks created by rep
  • Completed Tasks - Tasks finished
  • Task Completion % - Follow-through rate
  • Total Events - Meetings, demos scheduled
  • Activity Volume - Overall activity count

5. Gaming Detection Flags

  • Ghost Dialer - >40% calls under 5 seconds
  • VM Rambler - High talk time, low meaningful calls
  • Volume No Outcome - Many calls, zero deals
  • Fake Busy - High activity, zero pipeline
  • Low Follow Through - Tasks created but never completed
